Inglemoor High School

One of Inglemoor's greatest strengths is developing students to be well-versed in the field of technology.

Inglemoor High School

Inglemoor High School is a highly successful IB school located in the beautiful Pacific Northwest. Our school is known for academic and competitive success within clubs and organizations like TSA, as well as the creation of award winning projects from CTE courses like IB Design Tech.

CTE Courses

Inglemoor High School has a variety of school courses that can be taken by all students at Inglemoor. There are courses ranging from programming classes to design classes to engineering classes, all at varying degrees of difficulty level to remain accessible to all students.

IB Computer Science With Java SL

This course will establish a fundamental base knowledge of Java. Students will learn about objects, classes, Java class libraries, and much more. Additional topics will be studied, such as software development and user interface design. The class culminates with an internal assessment that takes place over the course of the second semester, in which students must find a client to develop software for, gaining experience about all parts of the development process.

Web Design

In this course, students will learn the process of designing and developing web pages. Students will become familiar and proficient with HTML, CSS, and Photoshop in order to hone their web design skills.

IB Design and Technology SL/HL

These two courses focus on designing projects based around human factors, sustainability, materials, and teach skills within CAD and prototyping. Students will have the opportunity to work on many projects, and will develop portfolios showcasing their work.

Introduction to Engineering Design

This hands-on course will explore the process that goes into building new things. Students will study manufacturing, CAD, structures, and many other topics that will develop new skills, as well as developing existing skills in order to apply them to the many projects that are completed in this course.

Academics at Inglemoor

IB Program:

Inglemoor has a high graudation rate and many students choose to pursue the full IB diploma. The Diploma Programme (DP) curriculum is made up of six subject groups and the DP core, comprising theory of knowledge (TOK), creativity, activity, service (CAS) and the extended essay. Through the Diploma Programme (DP) core, students reflect on the nature of knowledge, complete independent research and undertake a project that often involves community service.

AP Program:

Many students also choose to do the AP program. "Advanced Placement is a program in the United States and Canada created by the College Board which offers college-level curricula and examinations to high school students. American colleges and universities may grant placement and course credit to students who obtain high scores on the examinations. The AP curriculum for each of the various subjects is created for the College Board by a panel of experts and college-level educators in that field of study."
