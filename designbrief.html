Music

John Wick has spent the last few years of his life learning how to DJ and create different types of music. He treats music production as a hobby but wants to turn it into a career, and hopes to major in music in college. He also enjoys being a part of the school band and hopes to be a drum major his senior year,

Community

John Wick is heavily involved within his school and community at large. Wick spends a lot of time volunteering and is very dedicated to helping others; recently agreed to DJ for a local 5k event that will be raising money for a local soup kitchen, using his talents to directly benefit the others around him.
